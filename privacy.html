Privacy Notice and Notice at Collection

Privacy Notice

Last updated: June 18, 2026

This notice explains how Evan Karwowski Real Estate and Karwowski, Inc. may collect, use, disclose, and protect information submitted through this website. This template should be reviewed by your broker and a qualified attorney before launch.

Information collected

When you submit a form, we may collect your name, email address, phone number, area or city of interest, timeline, message content, consent selections, and technical information connected to the submission.

If you accept optional tracking, this website may use cookies, local storage, and a Meta Pixel to collect information such as pages visited, buttons clicked, approximate device and browser information, and whether a lead form was submitted.

We do not intentionally collect Social Security numbers, bank account information, credit card numbers, precise geolocation, health information, or other sensitive personal information through this website.

Purposes for collection

  • Responding to your inquiry
  • Providing requested real estate education or resources
  • Scheduling calls or follow up conversations
  • Improving website performance and content
  • Measuring advertising and retargeting audiences, only when optional tracking is accepted
  • Maintaining records of consent and communications
  • Complying with legal, regulatory, brokerage, and security obligations

Sharing

We do not sell your contact information. We may share information with service providers that help operate the website, process forms, host data, manage email, measure analytics, or provide advertising tools. We may also share information with the responsible broker, transaction support providers, lenders, escrow, title, inspectors, attorneys, or other professionals when you ask us to connect you or when it is necessary for a real estate transaction.

California privacy choices

Depending on whether the California Consumer Privacy Act applies to the business, California residents may have rights to know, delete, correct, opt out of sale or sharing, limit sensitive information use, and avoid discrimination for exercising privacy rights.

You can opt out of optional website tracking by selecting Reject in the cookie banner, by using the Do Not Sell or Share My Personal Information link in the footer, or by using a browser-based Global Privacy Control signal.

To submit a privacy request, email evan@thewowski.com with the subject line Privacy Request.

Retention and security

We keep inquiry and consent information only as long as reasonably necessary for the purposes described above, unless a longer period is required for legal, brokerage, tax, dispute, or transaction recordkeeping reasons. No website can guarantee perfect security, but reasonable administrative, technical, and organizational safeguards should be used.

Children

This website is intended for adults and is not directed to children under 16. Do not submit information through this website if you are under 16.
