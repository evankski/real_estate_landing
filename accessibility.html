Accessibility Statement

Last updated: June 15, 2026

Evan Karwowski Real Estate aims to make this website accessible and usable for all visitors. The site includes semantic HTML, alt text for meaningful images, keyboard accessible navigation, a skip link, visible focus states, and reduced motion support.

Need help?

If you experience difficulty using this website, email evan@thewowski.com with the page, issue, browser, and assistive technology used if applicable. We will make reasonable efforts to provide the information in another format.
