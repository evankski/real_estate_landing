What to understand first

Before you buy in Brentwood

Brentwood sits in Contra Costa County within the Highway 4 corridor. The decision often comes down to space, newer homes, East Bay family logistics, commute reality, Mello Roos, insurance, and whether the extra square footage is worth the drive.

Questions worth answering

  • What does the commute look like on the actual days you drive?
  • How much do taxes, insurance, HOA, and Mello Roos change the payment?
  • Is the home newer because it fits your life, or because the listing photos look clean?
  • How close are groceries, parks, schools, medical care, and family support?
